About First Step Pre School & Primary School

First Step Pre School & Primary School is a trusted neighbourhood school in Saurabh Vihar, Jaitpur, Badarpur, New Delhi (110044) serving children from Playgroup, Nursery, LKG, UKG through Class 5. We focus on activity-based early learning, structured English & Hindi phonics, art & craft, music, dance, and supervised outdoor play in a safe, CCTV-monitored campus with small class sizes and caring trained teachers.

Admissions are open for the 2026-27 academic year. We are highly rated by local parents (4.9★ on Google) and serve families across Saurabh Vihar, Jaitpur, Badarpur, Molarband, Tajpul Pul and Sarita Vihar.
